Let's Play!Android App

This Android app was created as a freelance job in 2013 for a client who hired me through elance.com(now upwork.com). My job was to make a simple android app to go along with a recently published book that contained rules, guides, and other information on 100 different card/dice/dart/domino games. The app had to include all the information in the book, which the client provided me in the form of 100 html-formatted documents. I found a way to easily display his html files within the app, and also added a scoreboard feature that allowed app users to keep score in various different games. The client was very pleased with my work, leaving this feedback after I finished his app:

"Superb work! Very responsive, extensive subject matter knowledge, exacting attention to detail, and results exactly as represented. Would not hesitate to hire again!"
